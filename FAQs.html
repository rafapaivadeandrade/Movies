    Tickets purchased online can be refunded, modified or exchanged at the applicable theatre anytime in advance of the movie's showtime. Only refunds and exchanges requested in theatre prior to the ticketed show time are eligible and remain at the discretion of the management. Landmark Cinemas and its management reserves the right to refuse the refund or exchange of any admission ticket, including but not limited to tickets purchased from a third party seller.
