    About Us

    Acquired in January 2018 by Cinema Movies Enterprise is Canada’s second largest motion picture theatre exhibition company.

    We continue to provide the perfect setting for popcorn munching Movie Lovers to connect and share their love of movies at 35 theatres on 317 screens throughout Western Canada, Ontario and the Yukon Territory, in multiple formats that include Premium Large Format (PLF) brands (IMAX®, Extra, Xtreme), and RealD 3D technology, and in select locations in the premium comfort of Full-Recliner Seating and with the added convenience of FREE Reserved Seating.

    We are connected to the communities we serve, we are committed to support the mental health and well-being of both our youth Guests and Cast & Crew.
